Understanding Emotion Categories

Hope

Expressions of optimism, dreams, and positive expectations. These emotions naturally rise in the visualization, drawn upward by their inherent lightness, seeking connection with other uplifting feelings and creating networks of encouragement.

Sorrow

Expressions of sadness, loss, and melancholy that find solace in deeper spaces. These emotions cluster together, creating communities of understanding where people realize they're not alone in their struggles.

Transformative

Emotions of growth, change, and breakthrough moments. These display dynamic movement patterns, often serving as bridges between different emotional states and catalyzing evolution in their surroundings.

Ambivalent

Complex feelings of internal conflict and mixed emotions. These display intricate movement patterns that reflect the multifaceted nature of human experience and the reality that we often feel multiple things simultaneously.

Reflective

Contemplative and balanced emotions that serve as stabilizing forces in the emotional landscape. These provide calm centers for more intense emotions to orbit, representing moments of introspection and thoughtfulness.
